Reverse Engineered Tire Changing Kit


Skills Improved: Product Design, Mechanical & Electrical Design, Solidworks, Manufacture, User Groups

The EZ Car Tire Changing Kit was designed to meet the needs of users that may have had difficulty operating the reference design. To make the best product on the market, the EZ Car Tire Changing Kit had to target the three main issues with the current tire changing methods. To change a tire the user must first lift the car then remove the lug nuts off the tire, then remove the tire itself. The EZ Car Tire Changing Kit was not only better than the reference design at this operation but, the best in the market as it also accounts for portability, functionality, efficientancy, user-friendliness and the safety of its users.

The first task the user needed to do was lifting up the car. A standard jack requires the user to exert large amounts of physical force while being in a low position. This is ergonomically hard on the user as it forces them into uncomfortable positions for the entirety of the task. The Foot Operated Hydraulic Piston was designed to perform the functions of a car jack while allowing the user to stand. Once the piston had been positioned under the car, the user stands up and cranks the foot paddle on the piston. The pumping motion allows the hydraulic fluid in the piston to push the connecting rod in the upwards direction allowing the car to be lifted. After the car tire had been changed, the user simply has to step on the pressure release button on the piston to bring the car down. This design was also deemed to be safer, as there are no moving parts with cavities in them. On a scissor lift for example, there are many open areas where a finger could go in and due to a mechanical failure, injure the user. The piston does not allow for such possibilities, hence making it safer design to lift the car.

The second task after lifting the car was removing the lug nuts. A standard lug wrench requires the user to exert a considerable amount of force multiple times. This proves to be a difficult task for any user with injuries, muscular dystrophy or tremors. Furthermore, any user inadequately dressed would be unable to perform this task.The Semi-Automatic Drill solved this problem as it only requires the user to fasten the sockets onto the lug nuts, then simply perform the rest with a remote. The remote allows the user to perform three main operations; screw or unscrew the lug nuts, secondly based on the tire type, change the diameter between the sockets, and third stop the drill using the Emergency Stop button. This allows the user to operate the drill without compromising their safety and at the same time providing a user-friendly product. One of the main requirements was to have some automatic or semi-automatic components to aid users. The third task which may be difficult for the user was the removal of the tire from the tire shaft. Removing the tire requires the user to be bent over in a position that applies extreme levels of strain on their backs. Moreover, tires can be quite heavy for certain users or in some cases impossible to lift on their own. For these reasons, the dolly was designed to aid the user. The dolly simply rolls under the lifted tire, knocks it off the axle with a simple tilt, and gather the tire with ease.

Our design can be attached and stored together to avoid losing parts and at the same time reduce the storage capacity. Cargo space is a large priority for many users, thus, creating a compact design was an important design criteria. The semi automatic drill connects to the front of our dolly with heavy weight velcro, while the piston latches onto the back of the dolly. The sinking of the handle to reduce the height of th dolly along with the foldable hooks make the dolly compact. This design feature allows all the components of the kit to be paired together while staying in one compact spot in the userâ€™s car. Instead of being in one large box like our reference design, being connected but exposed allows the user to disassemble it in the car. This is relevant as the user will never have to carry the full weight of the kit at once. Instead, they can take it apart and just carry one component at a time.

Other features were added to our kit to make it truly stand out. The most important being the semi-automatic drill with magnetic sockets and its ability to change the diameter between the sockets. By having the sockets on a groove connected to a belt, the distance between the socket and the center of the drill can be varied. The magnetic sockets eliminates the possibility of losing the lug nuts during the tire changing process. These features allow for an adaptable and user-friendly product.

The dolly has bright colored reflective tape. This allows the dolly to be seen by oncoming traffic, similar to a reflective vest. Most of the components are made using rubber and aluminium which decreases the chances of rust. Additionally, the entire kit has sacrificial anodes applied to prevent rust. The dolly also has rubber treaded wheels which allow the dolly to be used on multiple terrains. Lastly, the dolly is sturdy enough to be used for other activities as well in the users day to day life.

What really made this design stand out compared to other embodiments was the fact that it performed the same function as our number 2 and 3 spots, but better. This final design was the safest and resulted in the user being in the least compromising positions, baring the least amount of weight and using the least force. This final design is the most functional and efficient way concept which reflected upon our groups requirements as stated on page 32.

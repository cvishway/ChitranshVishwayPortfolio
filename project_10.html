Project ReStore - Shipping Container Homes


Skills Improved: Product Development, Engineering Design, Design-for-Customer, Social Entrepreneurship

Project restore was a social enterprise with a mission to recycle shipping containers into homes for marginalized communities. As the Lead Engineering Associate on the project, I was tasked with developement and validation of the engineering feasiblity of the project. This included but not limited to: cost analysis for materials, heating/cooling systems, electrial requirments, and CAD Design.

With this experience, I was able to utitlize my engineering knowledge for a better cause. As a team, we raised over $15,000 in seed funding by winning the Enactus Regional competition. Furthermore, by placing second at the National compeition, the team was able to raise awareness of the housing crisis in impoverished communities.
